ABOUT US





About Toa fitness

Toa fitness is not a fitness brand. It’s a lifestyle, a way of life. One that’s built on universal principles and a Greater Purpose. Every Choice You Make is either adding strength to you as an individual or stripping it from you! You must push yourself harder & harder. Sacrifice more & more to discover yourself and what you’re capable of. That’s what Toa fitness is all about. It’s for people who want more out of life and want to push themselves to become a better..no..THE BEST version of themselves.










About me

Sonny Jay Tonga - Coach SJ
If you told me 5 years ago I was going to be a personal development, and health and well-being coach I would’ve told you to shut the front door.
My passion for health and fitness truly started during a very dark time of my life I thought I would never escape, Until immersing myself into fitness.
Fitness was like my way out, my escape from reality. Fitness was the key to this prison I was trapped in. It was meditation for me and the start of everything I am today. The changes that happened physically, but most importantly the changes that happened mentally while training changed my whole outlook on life.
Fast forward to today and on my journey to becoming my highest self, I came to the realisation that my purpose is beyond me trying to better myself, but me trying to help others become the best version the can be one step at a time.
Are you willing to break though the chains that are holding you back !
