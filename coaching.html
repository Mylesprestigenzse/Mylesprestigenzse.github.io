THE WARRIOR BLUEPRINT

The warrior blueprint is establishing a foundation in your life across all four pillars (HAUORA), and is committed to achieving super human status = meaning I am going to be fit spiritually and mentally while also being emotionally connected - putting life together under the art of having it all.

Taha Tinana (Physical Well-Being - Health)

Taha Hinengaro (Mental and Emotional Well-Being - Self-Confidence)

Taha Whanau (Social Well-Being - Self-Esteem)

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Well-Being - Personal Beliefs)
